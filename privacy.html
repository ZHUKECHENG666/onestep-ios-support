Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

OneStep is a simple and lightweight expense tracking app designed for local use. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the iOS version of the app.

1. Account Registration

OneStep does not require account registration or login. The developer does not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or account information through the app.

2. Expense Data

Expense records entered in OneStep are stored locally on the user's device. The developer does not upload, collect, view, or share the user's expense records.

3. Import and Export

OneStep may provide import and export features for local JSON files. When using these features, the app only accesses files selected by the user for importing or exporting data.

4. Advertising

OneStep may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain data, such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction data, crash logs, and performance data, for advertising, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement purposes.

For more information about how Google handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy .

5. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell or share users' personal expense records with third parties. If advertising services are used, data may be processed by the advertising provider according to its own policies.

6. Children's Privacy

OneStep is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
