Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

OneStep is a simple and lightweight expense tracking app designed for local use. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the iOS version of the app.

1. Account Registration

OneStep does not require account registration or login. The developer does not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or account information through the app.

2. Expense Data

Expense records entered in OneStep are stored locally on the user's device. The developer does not upload, collect, view, sell, or share the user's personal expense records.

3. Import and Export

OneStep may provide import and export features for local JSON files. When using these features, the app only accesses files selected by the user for importing or exporting data. The developer does not receive or store imported or exported files.

4. Advertising

OneStep may display advertisements, including advertisements on the home screen, through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These advertising services may collect or process certain data, such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction data, crash logs, diagnostic data, and performance data. Such data may be used for advertising, ad measurement, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement purposes.

Advertising services process data according to their own privacy policies. For more information about how Google handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy .

5. In-App Purchases

OneStep may offer optional in-app purchases, such as a feature to remove advertisements. All in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

The developer does not collect, store, or have access to the user's payment card information, billing information, Apple ID password, or other payment details.

The app may use purchase status or transaction-related information provided by Apple only to unlock purchased features, restore purchases, and prevent unauthorized access to paid features.

6. Remove Ads

If the user purchases the remove ads feature, advertisements will no longer be displayed in the app after the purchase is successfully verified.

Please note that third-party advertising services may have processed certain data before the purchase was completed or before advertisements were removed.

7. Tracking and User Consent

Depending on the user's region, device settings, and advertising configuration, OneStep or third-party advertising services may request consent for personalized advertising, tracking, or access to advertising identifiers.

Users can manage app tracking permission in iOS Settings. Where required by law or platform rules, users may also be provided with options to manage advertising consent.

8. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell or share users' personal expense records with third parties. If advertising services are used, certain data may be processed by the advertising provider according to its own privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

OneStep is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
