OneStep Support

OneStep is a lightweight expense tracking app designed for fast and simple daily recording.

Features

  • Fast expense recording from the home screen
  • Customizable expense category buttons
  • Simple and lightweight interface
  • Local data storage
  • JSON import and export support

About

OneStep does not require account registration or login. All records are stored locally on the user's device.
